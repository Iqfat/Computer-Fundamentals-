Introduction

This report analyses our customer data using a variety of graphs and charts created with Power BI. Our goal is to find significant trends and patterns in consumer spending patterns, credit ratings, and demographics. We intend to make more informed decisions that enhance our business operations and strategies by having a better understanding of these factors. All visualisations in this report are designed to make complex data easily comprehensible, assisting us in identifying key areas for improvement and action.

Analysis

Stacked Bar Chart

Analysing:

  • Customer Distribution by City
  • Gender Segmentation

Insights: City Dominance, Targeted Campaigns

Clustered Column Chart

Analysing:

  • City-wise Income Distribution
  • Gender Disparity in Income

Insights: Identify High and Low-Income Areas, Insight into Market Segmentation

Scatter Chart

This chart is to identify patterns or correlations between a customer's credit score and their spending behaviour.

Insights: Marketing Strategies, Risk Management, Customer Segmentation

Pie Chart

This chart visually represents how your customer base is distributed across various cities.

Insights: Customer Concentration, Market Potential

Matrix

This matrix focuses on analysing the distribution of customers across different cities and genders.

Insights: Customer Distribution Across Cities, Gender Distribution within Cities, Balanced vs. Skewed Distribution

Overall Strategic Decisions

  • Marketing Strategy Enhancement
  • Resource Optimization
  • Product and Service Customization
  • Customer Engagement and Retention

Conclusion

A businessman may create a comprehensive business strategy that maximizes their operations, marketing, and product offerings across various geographies and client categories by combining the data from all the charts. With the ultimate goal of increasing profitability and market visibility, this strategy ensures that decisions are data-driven and take into account the intricate dynamics of your clientele.
